METAXENOPY.YT respects your privacy. This page explains the information that may be collected when you browse the site, react to posts, leave comments, or contact the publisher directly.

This website is published through GitHub Pages and may also use third-party services for search verification, advertising operations, and analytics-related features. The goal of this page is to explain those relationships clearly.

## Information Collected

- Basic technical request data may be processed by website infrastructure and third-party services such as GitHub Pages, Google Search Console, and Google AdSense.
- If you use the contact form, your name, email address, message, and limited request metadata are submitted to this site's self-hosted engagement and inquiry API so the message can be reviewed and answered.
- If you leave a blog comment, your name, email hash, comment content, and limited technical request metadata are stored through the site's self-hosted engagement API.
- If you react to a post or load a tracked article, the site may store a visitor token, aggregate reaction counts, and visitor-count data through the self-hosted engagement API.

## How Information Is Used

- To deliver website content and keep the site working properly.
- To understand general search visibility and technical issues through tools like Google Search Console.
- To respond to direct inquiries submitted by visitors.
- To moderate and display blog comments.
- To understand aggregate post engagement such as visit counts and reactions.
- To support advertising operations if Google AdSense or similar services are active on the site.

## Cookies and Advertising

This site may use Google AdSense to display ads. Google and its partners may use cookies to personalize advertising and measure performance. Visitors can learn more about how Google uses information here:

[How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

The site may use Google Privacy & messaging / Funding Choices for European regulations messages, US state privacy messages, and ad blocking recovery. Where those Google messages are shown, they are the primary place to manage Google advertising consent and opt-out signals.

The site also includes a local privacy choices panel. Essential site behavior remains active, but optional analytics and advertising storage can be accepted, rejected, or customized. If you reject optional advertising storage, the site treats that as an opt-out of personalized advertising and sale/share-style advertising signals where those concepts apply under US state privacy laws.

You can reopen these settings from the **Privacy choices** link in the footer.

## Search and Performance Data

Google Search Console may be used to understand search performance, indexing status, and technical issues related to this website. If analytics consent is allowed, the site may also send privacy-conscious performance measurements such as Core Web Vitals events to help diagnose loading speed and responsiveness.

## Third-Party and Self-Hosted Services

- GitHub Pages may process technical request data needed to serve the site.
- Google services may process limited technical, analytics, or advertising-related data where enabled.
- A self-hosted engagement API processes limited technical request data to store post visit counts, reactions, post metadata, comments, and contact inquiries.

## Content and Embedded Media

Some pages may embed or link to third-party content such as YouTube videos. Embedded videos are generally loaded only after you choose to play them. Those platforms may collect their own technical or usage information according to their own policies when you interact with that content.

## Your Choices

- You can choose not to submit the contact form if you do not want to share your contact information.
- You can choose not to leave a comment or reaction on blog posts.
- You can accept, reject, or customize optional analytics and advertising storage through the footer **Privacy choices** control.
- You can turn off ads in the privacy choices panel to opt out of personalized advertising and sale/share-style advertising signals on this site.
- You can review Google privacy and advertising controls directly through your Google account and browser settings.
- You can contact the publisher if you need clarification about information submitted through this website.
