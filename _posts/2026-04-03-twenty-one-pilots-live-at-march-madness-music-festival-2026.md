# Twenty One Pilots - March Madness Music Festival (AT&T Block Party)

Watch **Twenty One Pilots** headline the AT&T Block Party during the NCAA March Madness Music Festival held in Indianapolis, Indiana.

The performance took place on **Friday, April 3**, where the duo closed the opening night of the festival with a high-energy set at American Legion Mall. Known for their dynamic live shows, Tyler Joseph and Josh Dun delivered an immersive performance featuring fan favorites, transitions, and live-exclusive segments.
