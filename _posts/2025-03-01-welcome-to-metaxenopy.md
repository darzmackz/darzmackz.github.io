Welcome to **METAXENOPY.YT**. This site is the main home for my work under the name **KENJI** and the creator brand **METAXENOPY**. I built it to do three things clearly: show who is behind the content, give readers a better archive than a social feed can provide, and connect my music work with my professional background in systems support.

## Why This Site Exists

Most platforms are good for publishing quickly but not for explaining context. A YouTube channel can host the video, but it does not give much room for background, related links, or a broader picture of the person maintaining the work. This site fills that gap.

Here, I can keep a cleaner public archive, point visitors to the right sections, and publish posts that are worth reading even without hitting play on a video first.

## About Me

I am **KENJI**, also known as **Kent Harvey Plando**. My day job is in system analysis, database support, troubleshooting, deployments, and server administration. Alongside that work, I create lyric and karaoke content for people who want clear, usable music videos instead of cluttered or hard-to-follow uploads.

That mix of creator work and technical work shapes how I run this site. I want the pages to be straightforward, useful, and maintained with intention.

The public archive is intentionally selective. If a post feels unfinished, repetitive, or too thin to stand on its own, I would rather leave it unpublished than use it as filler.

## What METAXENOPY Focuses On

METAXENOPY started from lyric videos and karaoke uploads, especially the kind of songs people actively want to revisit, practice, or share. Over time, the site has also become a place for wider music-related writing, such as artist features, local music highlights, and long-form fandom guides when there is enough substance to justify them.

That is why the archive can include both a Cebu-centered local music post and a deep dive into a band like Twenty One Pilots. The common thread is not chasing every possible keyword. It is publishing around the music and topics I actually spend time with.
